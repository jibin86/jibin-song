About

Building generative models for visual and multimodal media.

I am currently an M.S. student in the Department of Artificial Intelligence at Yonsei University and a graduate researcher at Visual Intelligence Lab, advised by Prof. Yongjung Uh.

My research interests lie in generative AI, especially diffusion-based video generation, audio-conditioned generation, video editing, and efficient sampling methods for high-quality synthesis.
